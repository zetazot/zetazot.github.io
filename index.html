About Me

I specialize in brand identity, print production, and publication design. For over 15 years I have worked as a designer in both agency and in-house marketing teams, as well as freelance. I have defined and unified brand standards across both print and digital platforms for organizations such as Briggs and Stratton, the EarQ Group, and various freelance clients. I have experience organizing and directing photoshoots for use as marketing collateral in catalog publication, direct mail, and digital assets. In addition, I have worked with print vendors through the production process, from quotes to press checks to distribution.

A company’s brand is what defines it — I strive to make that brand shine and to ensure that it is recognizable wherever it is advertised.

Work

Design is about communication and making connections between people. A good graphic design moves people. It can bring them joy or sadness. It makes them think and to reflect on what is presented to them.

As a graphic designer, I focus on concept design, development, and execution in many different contexts and media. When creating a client’s logo for example, I will consider how it will appear in the different contexts such as web, mono printing, and large scale banner ads. This is essential to create a design that will not only look great in various contexts but be adaptable to each.
